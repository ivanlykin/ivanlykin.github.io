What we do

What we do

VIO Organisation is an outsourcing direct marketing company that provides customers and sales solutions for profit and non-profit organisations via our human commercial.

Today large and well-known companies are changing from traditional marketing to direct marketing. This is because people are exposed to more than 3000 advertising messages every day, however only small percentage is perceived. Companies that are looking for good customer flow require more effective way of customer attraction that guarantees good return on investment and right target audience. That’s why our company exists. We are professionals in face-to-face marketing and our strength in human commercial allows us to reach target markets and secure thousands of new customers for our clients. Our three main routes to market are B2B, residence and events.
